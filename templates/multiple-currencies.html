Support for Multiple Currencies

Our Expense Tracker application supports multiple currencies, allowing users to manage their expenses in various formats. This feature enables users to input expenses in their local currencies, making it easier to track spending across different regions.

How It Works

When adding an expense, users can select their preferred currency from a dropdown list. The application will convert the amount to a default currency for consistency in reporting and analysis.

Supported Currencies

The following currencies are currently supported:

  • USD - United States Dollar
  • EUR - Euro
  • GBP - British Pound
  • JPY - Japanese Yen
  • AUD - Australian Dollar
  • CAD - Canadian Dollar
  • INR - Indian Rupee
  • CHF - Swiss Franc

FAQs

How are conversion rates determined?

Conversion rates are updated regularly based on market data to ensure accuracy.

Can I switch currencies after entering an expense?

Yes, you can edit an expense to change the currency at any time.
